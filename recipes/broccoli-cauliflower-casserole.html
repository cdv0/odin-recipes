Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

Description

A delicious and nutritious baked dish that combines broccoli and cauliflower with a creamy sauce and cheesy topping. It's topped off with a layer of crushed croutons to give a crunchy dimension.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 15 ounce package seasoned croutons, crushed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Boil broccoli and cauliflower in different saucepans for 3 to 4 minutes until tender. Drain and arrange the vegetables in an 11x7 inch baking dish.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl until creamy. Stir in 1/2 cup of Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion and pour over vegetables. Pour melted butter on top and sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese. Scatter crushed croutons evenly on top.
  4. Bake the dish in the oven for approcimately 40 minutes until brown and bubbling.
